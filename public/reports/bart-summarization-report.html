BART Summarization Pipeline

This report covers the encoder-decoder NLP workflow implemented in the Assignment 3 notebook using the XSum dataset and the facebook/bart-base checkpoint.

Transformers BART XSum ROUGE

What the project is about

The notebook studies whether continuing self-supervised denoising training on article text improves downstream summarization quality. It compares a domain-adapted checkpoint against the original pretrained model while keeping the supervised fine-tuning stage aligned across both runs.

How it was created

The project follows a three-stage sequence: load XSum and BART, continue masked denoising pretraining on article text, then fine-tune two models on summarization and evaluate them with ROUGE plus generated examples. The report image is derived from those notebook stages and reflects the exact progression used in the implementation.
